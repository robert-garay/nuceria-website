## Note on Brand Color

The original UI proposal suggested retiring the #2E3192 deep indigo in favor of a champagne gold/espresso palette. **This was rejected by the client** who wants to retain the existing brand identity.

**APPROVED BRAND COLOR**: #2E3192 (Deep Indigo) — see design-system.md for complete approved palette.

---

## Aesthetic Concept (Retained Principles)

Nuceria Health sits at the intersection of **clinical authority and Miami luxury** — a place where science-backed medicine meets the aspirational lifestyle of South Florida. The visual direction channels the feeling of a high-end private clinic: sophisticated, warm, and unmistakably professional.

**Core principles retained**:
- Maximum 2 font families (performance + elegance)
- Clean spacing and intentional white space
- Subtle animations that respect prefers-reduced-motion
- Premium aesthetic without being cold or clinical

## Retained Design Decisions

### Typography System (APPROVED)
- **Display**: Cormorant Garamond (serif)
- **Body**: DM Sans (sans-serif)
- Maximum 2 font families

### Spacing & Layout (APPROVED)
- 4px grid system
- 8px default border radius, 16px for cards
- Container max: 1280px

### Animation Philosophy (APPROVED)
- Calm authority principle
- 150ms for hover states
- 400-500ms for entrance animations
- Respects prefers-reduced-motion
